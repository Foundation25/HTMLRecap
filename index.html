Ingredients

  • Tomatoe
  • Rice
  • Red pepper
  • Seasoning: salt, maggi, bay leaf,,thyme, curry, ginger and garlic.
  • Onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Butter
  • Eggs for garnishing

Preparation:

Below is how to get your ingredients ready:

  1. Wash tomatoe and red pepper.
  2. Peel outer skin if Onion, ginger, and garlic ; wash afterwards.
  3. Blend tomatoe, Onion, pepper, ginger, and garlic
